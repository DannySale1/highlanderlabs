Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from researchers.

What are research peptides?

Research peptides are short chains of amino acids used for laboratory, in vitro, or analytical science work. They are not approved for human or veterinary use, and are intended solely for experimental or research purposes.

How should the peptides be stored?

Peptides are best kept in cool, dry conditions. Lyophilized (powder) form should be stored as indicated on the product page and protected from moisture. After reconstitution, use according to lab‑specific guidance.

How long does shipping take?

Orders placed by 3 PM EST ship the same or next business day. Delivery times depend on the destination; most U.S. orders arrive within 3 business days with standard service.

What is your refund / return policy?

Because these are specialized research compounds, returns are not accepted once a product has left the facility, unless there is an error on our part or the product fails quality control. Contact our team with your order and batch/lot info to request a COA review.

Are you responsible for how your peptides are used?

No. All compounds sold by Highlander Labs are for laboratory research use only and are not intended for human or veterinary consumption. Highlander Labs does not condone, support, or accept any misuse of its products. By purchasing, you agree that you are solely responsible for how these compounds are handled, stored, and used.
